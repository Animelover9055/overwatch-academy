Coaching Services

Choose a training option based on what you want to improve: mechanics, game sense, role knowledge, or team coordination.

Available Coaching Packages

VOD Review

Submit a recorded match and receive feedback on positioning, cooldowns, decision-making, and team fight mistakes. 45-minute session.

Aim Training Plan

Personalized drills to improve tracking, flicks, crosshair placement, reaction time, and hero-specific mechanics.

Role Coaching

One-on-one coaching for tank, damage, or support players. Focuses on role responsibility, map positioning, and match impact.

Rank Climb Plan

A weekly improvement plan that identifies your biggest weaknesses and gives you clear goals for ranked matches.

Team Strategy Session

Designed for groups or esports clubs. Covers communication, team compositions, ultimate economy, and match review.

Beginner Bootcamp

A starter program for new players covering hero roles, basic settings, map awareness, and how competitive mode works.
